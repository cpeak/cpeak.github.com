Gallery Experimentations

There's a tension here between letting the users upload whatever image they want in their original aspect ratios vs. us wanting the cleanest grids possible. As of right now (Oct 3) I don't see a way to 100% guarantee no gaps. I think the key is us locking the presentation of the artwork to the standard european A paper size ratios, that way both horizontal and portrait based images work well together.

Below are some experimental grids using both Masonry and just pure CSS. The goals here are to have the most minimal amount of code. The galleries are all using the same image set in the same order, a mix of horizontal, vertical, square, and using different aspect ratios as well.
