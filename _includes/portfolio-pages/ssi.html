SSI Agvance SKY

In the fall of 2022 I was brought onboard as a senior designer to help out SSI with their effort to migrate a legacy windows application into a modern web based platform called Agvance SKY. The application has a number of functions, but my area was focused on providing services enabling Farmers and Merchandisers to handle contracts for the purchase, selling, and transportation of grain.

Understanding the business model and gaining trust

The first hurdle was to understand the business model - simply put getting grain from the farm to food manufacturers is a symphony of labor and contracts. Getting an understanding of all the moving parts was like drinking from the firehose. The second hurdle was establishing trust with SSI in order to get them out of the feature parity mindset. Their focus was simply on taking the Windows application and moving it to a web app, with little to no effort on improvement on User Experience.
