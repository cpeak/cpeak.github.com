This week's win(s)

A few months ago a book slid down and knocked my FFVII Aerith statuette off of the floating shelf in my office. Unfortunately she was damaged pretty significantly losing both arms, a leg, and her hair. I debating straight up replacing it, but I realized I had bought it back in the early 00s and the only ones I could find on eBay were well north of $250 dollars. So it was time to get out the super glue. While I got her limbs back on, there just wasn't enough surface area to glue her pony tail back on. After some research and discussion with friends, I learned that I could use a tiny drill called a pin vice, to open up a small hole and use a piece of a paperclip to hold everything together.

I very gently drilled a hole with the pin vice and inserted a small piece of paperclip. I drilled another hole in the back of her ponytail, then added superglue and used a twist-tie to hold it in place for 24 hours (just to be sure). Came out probably stronger than before, so thats a win.
