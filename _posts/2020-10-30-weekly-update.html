This was a challenging week, but I'm looking ahead to more positive things.

This week's win(s)

I'd say the biggest win was getting the recumbent in shape and out for more than a spin around the block. I was able to do a 14 mile round trip to the donut store and back. I was also able to get the kitchen pantry drawers installed, and started the edge banding to clean it all up. I'll share some photos next week.

This week's challenges

The big challenges this week were all work related, there was a few turbulent few days on my client project. Things were shifting fast, and there were a number of frankly very frustrating days. I powered through and believe things will get better, gotta stay positive.

Learn with me

I don't know if it will be 2021, but I really would love to do RAGBRAI. It's the Register's Annual Great Bike Ride Across Iowa, where 20k bike riders go from the Missouri River east to the Mississippi River, having fun along the way. 7 full days will be difficult, but I'd love to go one of these years.
