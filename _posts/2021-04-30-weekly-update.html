This week's challenges

Nothing too bad this week, however I did have one of those facepalm moments. The developer on the project and I were pairing, trying to track down why a certain toolbar was not showing up. He had worked on it for a while, then pinged me to talk through some ideas. We spent probably 15 or 20 minutes trying to figure out what was happening and the whole time I had something stuck in the back of my brain that I was forgetting something I did a few weeks ago. Long story short I had set a display: none on the toolbar while overhauling the layout. I deleted that one line of code, let out some choice swear words, and then moved on.
