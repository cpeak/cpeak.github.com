About

My name is Chris Peak and I am a design resident at 8th Light in Chicago, IL. I have been doing web development and graphic design for the better part of 10 years. My interest in computers started when I was tall enough to reach my dads Apple II, but my professional growth started when I wanted to create a website for my growing photography portfolio Gorilla-Studios in 2003. I worked as an Soda Jerk, Bartender, then an IT Technician for number of years before coming to 8th Light.

This blog is about my journey and experiences in web design and development. When I am not writing long-winded blog posts and staring at code all day I love photography, relaxing with my lovely wife, learning to juggle, and playing the guitar very poorly.
